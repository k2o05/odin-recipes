Karak Chai

Karak Chai and its History!

Karak chai is a style of masala chai that translates from Hindi to “strong tea.” It’s rich and creamy thanks to evaporated milk and is usually made with fewer spices than masala chai. Recipes vary family to family and some may include saffron.

I think this recipe is a happy medium. It has just enough spices to be comforting, warming and delicious without being overpowering. Karak tea is simple to make, but such a delicious tea to drink!

Ingredients!

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Tea
  • Ginger
  • Milk
  • Spices

Steps!

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add the teabags, cardamom, cinnamon, sugar, and vanilla extract and return to a boil.
  3. Stir carefully.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer till thick and creamy and caramel in color!
  5. Enjoy!
